# Booking and approval policy

## Lead times
- Small events (under 60 people): request at least **10 days** in advance.
- Large events (60+ people) or any event in the Atrium or Auditorium: at least **30 days**.
- Rooftop Terrace bookings must include a booked indoor backup space.

## Approval flow
1. The organizer submits an event request with date, space, expected headcount, and purpose.
2. Operations reviews availability and checks for conflicts with existing reservations.
3. The owning tenant of the space (if any) must approve the request.
4. Management gives final sign-off for events over 200 people or with paid ticketing.

## What makes a bid likely to be approved
- A clear purpose that fits the owning tenant's mission.
- Headcount within the space capacity, with a realistic layout.
- No conflict with the tenant's own programming on that date.
- A named on-site contact and a basic risk note (crowd, power, noise).

## Hard rules
- No open flame in the Atrium.
- Quiet hours in the Gallery after 21:00.
- Studio A is reserved for TUMO Tirana on weekdays until 18:00.
- Any event serving alcohol needs Management approval and a security plan.
