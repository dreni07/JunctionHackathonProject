# Pyramid of Tirana — bookable spaces

The Pyramid has several spaces that can be reserved for events. Capacities are for a
standard seated layout unless noted.

## Atrium (Ground floor)
The large central hall under the glass slope. Capacity 400 standing, 220 seated.
Best for exhibitions, launches, and receptions. Has natural light and step seating.
Load-in is via the north entrance. No open flame allowed.

## Auditorium (Level 1)
A 180-seat raked auditorium with a stage, projector, and full PA system. Best for
talks, screenings, panels, and conferences. Includes a green room and a control booth.

## Studio A (Level 2)
A flexible 60-person workshop room with movable tables and a 4K wall display. Best for
classes, hackathons, and training. Managed by TUMO Tirana on weekdays until 18:00.

## Gallery (Level 2)
A 120 m² white-wall gallery for exhibitions and installations. Hanging system on all
walls, track lighting. Managed by the ARTS tenant. Quiet hours apply after 21:00.

## Rooftop Terrace
Outdoor terrace, capacity 150 standing. Best for evening receptions and summer events.
Weather-dependent; a wet-weather backup space must be booked alongside it.

## Co-work Loft (Level 3)
Open-plan space with 40 desks, used by the ICT Ecosystem tenant. Available for demo
days and meetups outside core working hours (after 18:00 on weekdays, anytime weekends).
