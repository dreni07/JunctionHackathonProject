# Tenant event preferences

The Pyramid is shared by three tenants. Tailoring an event bid to the owning tenant's
aims greatly increases the chance of approval.

## TUMO Tirana (education)
Free after-school tech and design program for teens. Favors workshops, hackathons,
maker sessions, and student showcases. Owns Studio A. Prefers daytime and early-evening
events that involve or inspire young learners. Dislikes purely commercial events during
program hours.

## ICT Ecosystem (technology)
Startups, companies, and innovation spaces. Favors demo days, meetups, product launches,
and hackathons. Owns the Co-work Loft. Open to evening and weekend events. Values events
that bring together founders, engineers, and investors.

## ARTS (culture)
Exhibitions, performances, and cultural programming. Owns the Gallery. Favors openings,
installations, screenings, and concerts. Cares about quiet hours and careful handling of
the exhibition walls. Prefers events with a clear artistic or cultural angle.
